The Running (G)lover

Hi everyone! My name is Jaclyn Glover, and I am a university administrator, nonprofit professional, and running enthusiast based in Washington, DC.

I started running two years ago to lose weight and get in shape in preparation for my sister's wedding. I ran my first mile ever on May 21, 2015. That following November, I ran my first 5K race. In March 2016, I ran a 10K, and then a 10-miler followed in April 2016. In September 2016, I completed my first half marathon. My goal is to run a full marathon in 2018 (race and date TBD), and this site will detail my training leading up to that race.

You can learn more about my running exploits on my Strava profile. Thank you for reading!
